About Andrea

You probably don't need a new dashboard.

You need a better one.

I'm a Brisbane-based data analyst who helps businesses transform their Power BI dashboards from cluttered and confusing into clear, high-performing tools. Most consultancies focus on building dashboards from scratch — I specialise in taking what you've already started and refining it.

That means cleaner visuals, smarter DAX logic, and professional layouts that feel like an app, not a spreadsheet. Whether it's fixing broken measures, streamlining navigation, or aligning your dashboards with corporate branding, I'll help you make your data work harder and look sharper.

Frequently asked questions

I specialise in refining existing Power BI dashboards by enhancing visuals, optimising DAX logic, improving Power Query transformations, boosting performance and creating reports that feel polished and intuitive.

Absolutely. That's my speciality. I focus on taking an existing dashboard and making it cleaner, faster, easier to use and more visually engaging.

Yes. Colours, typography, layouts, navigation and report styling can all be aligned to your organisation's branding guidelines.
